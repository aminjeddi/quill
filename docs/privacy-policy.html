Privacy Policy

Quill — Daily Writing Prompts

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Quill handles your information.

Data We Collect

Quill does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All writing entries and app preferences are stored locally on your device and never leave it.

Data Storage

Your journal entries, writing category preference, and reminder settings are stored entirely on your device using local storage. No account is required to use Quill, and no data is sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

Quill does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, Quill uses your device's local notification system to send reminders at the time you choose. This does not involve any external servers or data transmission.

Children's Privacy

Quill does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the date below. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us at aminjeddica@gmail.com.
